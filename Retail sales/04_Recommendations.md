## ðŸ”¹ Business Problem 3: Top Product Categories
**Objective:** Optimize product management, promotions, and age-targeted strategies.

### Recommendation 3.1: Overall Product Performance
- Prioritize inventory and stock management for top-revenue categories: Electronics, Clothing, and Beauty.  
- Focus promotional campaigns on high-frequency categories (Clothing and Beauty) to drive repeat purchases.  
- Bundle high-value and high-frequency products to increase average transaction value.

### Recommendation 3.2: Age-Based Product Preferences
- Tailor marketing campaigns for specific age groups:  
  - Beauty & Clothing for younger customers  
  - Electronics for middle-aged customers  
- Adjust inventory placement and product assortment to match age-segment preferences.  
- Create age-targeted promotions and loyalty programs to enhance customer engagement.
