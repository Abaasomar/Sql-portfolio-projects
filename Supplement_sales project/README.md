## Key Insights

- **Discount Patterns:** The United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom offer the highest average discounts, highlighting a competitive, promotion-driven environment.  
- **Top Revenue Categories & Platforms:** Minerals and Vitamins sold on **iHerb** generate the highest revenue, outperforming other platforms such as Amazon and Walmart.  
- **Year-over-Year Revenue Growth:** Revenue trends show periods of growth and decline, allowing identification of high-performing years and potential declines.  
- **Platform Performance:** iHerb, Amazon, and Walmart generate the majority of revenue, with iHerb alone contributing **34.28% of total revenue**.
