# Somalia Financial Analysis – Recommendations

## 2.1: Average annual spending
**Insight Recap:** 2022 had the highest average spending.  
**Recommendation:** 
- Review allocation patterns to ensure funds are efficiently used.  
- Plan for sustained funding in critical sectors to avoid sudden spikes.  

## 2.2: Average yearly spending by ministry
**Insight Recap:** Labor & Social Affairs led in 2022; Defense dominated 2015–2021.  
**Recommendation:** 
- Monitor high-spending ministries to ensure transparency and effectiveness.  
- Allocate resources according to evolving government priorities and social needs.  

## 2.3: Highest spending expense categories
**Insight Recap:** Social benefits, social assistance, and transfers are the largest categories.  
**Recommendation:** 
- Conduct periodic audits to optimize social spending.  
- Ensure inter-governmental transfers support regional development effectively.  

## 2.4: Spending on internationally funded projects
**Insight Recap:** RCRF, PFM, and SFF received the most funding.  
**Recommendation:** 
- Track project performance to justify high allocations.  
- Consider diversifying funding to other impactful projects to avoid over-concentration.
