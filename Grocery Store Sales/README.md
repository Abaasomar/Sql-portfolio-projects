# Grocery Store Sales Analysis

This project analyzes grocery store transaction data to uncover insights about sales performance, discount strategies, customer loyalty, product diversity, and store-level profitability.  
The analysis is performed using **MySQL / SQL Workbench**.

---

## Project Overview

The main goals of this project are:  
- Understand which products and stores generate the most revenue.  
- Evaluate discount strategies and promotional effectiveness.  
- Identify products that drive customer loyalty.  
- Explore product variety across aisles and overall inventory.  
- Assess geographic performance and top-performing store locations.

By analyzing these patterns, actionable recommendations can be made to improve revenue, customer engagement, and operational efficiency.

## Business Problems Addressed

The analysis focuses on answering these key questions:

1. **Highest average transaction value (Product-Store combinations)**  
   Identify where the most revenue is generated per transaction and which products drive higher customer spending.

2. **Discount penetration across stores**  
   Evaluate store-level discount strategies and determine the efficiency of promotions.

3. **Customer loyalty**  
   Identify products that contribute most to loyalty points earned and encourage repeat purchases.

4. **Product diversity across aisles**  
   Explore inventory variety to highlight strong assortment areas and underrepresented aisles.

5. **Geographic coverage and store performance**  
   Assess total sales and average transaction values across locations to identify top-performing stores and improvement opportunities.

---

## Key Insights

- Certain products and stores generate significantly higher revenue per transaction, e.g., "Apples" at "Megamart West Side".  
- Discount strategies vary; QuickStop Market shows 100% discount penetration, while others are more selective.  
- High-loyalty products like "Cereal" and "Yogurt" drive repeat customer purchases.  
- Product diversity is concentrated in a few aisles (Bakery, Beverages, Canned Soda), while other aisles may need expanded variety.  
- Store performance varies geographically, with top performers like Greengrocer Plaza, Megamart Westside, and Freshmart Downtown.

---

## Recommendations

- **Focus inventory and promotions** on high-value product-store combinations to maximize revenue.  
- **Balance discount strategies** to maintain profitability while encouraging customer engagement.  
- **Enhance loyalty programs** for top-performing products and incentivize lower-loyalty items to boost engagement.  
- **Expand product variety** in underrepresented aisles to improve customer satisfaction and sales.  
- **Target high-performing stores** with strategic marketing and operational support, while improving lower-performing locations through tailored promotions and inventory optimization.

## Conclusion

This project provides a data-driven approach to understanding grocery store performance.  
The insights and recommendations enable strategic decision-making to improve sales, optimize inventory, enhance customer loyalty, and strengthen store operations across all locations.
